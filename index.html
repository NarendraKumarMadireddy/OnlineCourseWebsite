How does a fee refund work for the job guaranteed courses?

If you have successfully graduated from the Program by obtaining the final Certificate of Achievement issued by Manikanth Academy, activated the Career Services with Manikanth Academy, and have complied, without any violation/default, with the terms and conditions of Career Services including but not limited to, participated in all the mentoring and profile building sessions arranged for you, conducted a professional job search, appeared for all the interviews that you have been shortlisted for, and have not yet received a job offer for a Qualifying Position (defined) within the Career Service Period (defined), then you are eligible for refund and may request for such refund of Program tuition fee using the refund request by emailing at careerservices@manikanthacademy.net
The refund request form requires you to certify that you have met all the terms and conditions of this Program and have not been offered any job for Qualifying Positions (defined) within the Career Services Period (defined below). The refund request must be submitted within 30 (thirty) days after the end of your Career Services Period.
